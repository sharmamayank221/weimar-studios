About us

Weimar Studios is a multi purpose recording studio located at Weimar Institute in the heart of Northern California (Sierra Nevada Foothills).

Weimar Studios was created in the Spring of 2019 with the purpose of advocating the Christian message through producing quality, wholesome, and inspiring content while advancing the Weimar cause of Healing aHurting World.

Weimar Studios is dedicated to spreading messages of hope and healing through music and song as well as empowering lay people through the word of their testimony.

At Weimar Studios, we believe ensuring the quality of our content is founded on the Biblical principles found in Philippians 4:8 that , "whatever is true, whatever is honorable, whatever is just, whatever is pure, whatever is lovely, whatever is commendable , if there is any excellence, if there is anything worthy of praise, think about these things."
